About the AI e-HUB

Welcome to the SmAIle AI e-HUB, a central sustainable platform designed to develop core competencies in both teachers and students. Co-funded by the Erasmus+ Programme and developed by partners from five European countries, this project addresses the urgent need for ethical AI literacy in education.

Whether you are a teacher looking for a "Learning Scenario" to integrate into your class, or a student ready to take on an "AI Challenge," this open educational resource is for you.
