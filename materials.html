Welcome to the SmAIle Visual Library

Exploring Artificial Intelligence doesn't have to be overwhelming. As part of our commitment to accessible open educational resources (OER), the SmAIle project team has developed a comprehensive series of infographics and visual tutorials. They embody our core "3E" approach—focusing on the intersection of Ethics, Environment, and Education.
